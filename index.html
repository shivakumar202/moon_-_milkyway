Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about tours, bookings and logistics.

What should I bring to the stargazing tour?

Bring a light jacket, camera, and a charged phone. We provide telescopes and blankets.

Are tours suitable for children?

Yes — we have family-friendly sessions and child-safe telescope setups.

What happens in case of cloudy weather?

If skies are fully cloudy we offer rescheduling or a refund according to our booking policy.

How do I get to the meeting point?

Meeting point details are provided after booking; transport assistance can be arranged on request.
